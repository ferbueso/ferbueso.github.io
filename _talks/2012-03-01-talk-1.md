Spoke at the Health Data Science Program (HDS) — an organization dedicated to spreading data science and AI in the Hispanic American health sector — on the intersection of cybersecurity, AI, and digital transformation in LATAM healthcare systems.

Key themes explored:

- **Cybersecurity in healthcare** is no longer about protecting systems — it's about protecting clinical decisions
- **The cost of a breach** isn't only financial; it means delays, uncertainty, and risk for patients
- **As AI embeds deeper into clinical workflows**, risks stop being isolated and amplify across data, models, and decision pathways

Healthcare is now one of the most targeted and costly sectors for cyberattacks globally. AI, telemedicine, and connected devices are scaling faster than the security frameworks meant to hold them — making resilience a defining capability of modern health systems.
